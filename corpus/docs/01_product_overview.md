# Kestrel Systems — VoltEdge Product Overview

Kestrel Systems was founded in 2021 in San Diego and builds the VoltEdge line
of edge AI inference appliances for industrial computer vision.

The product line has three variants:

- **VoltEdge Nano** — fanless, DIN-rail mountable unit for single-camera
  deployments. Powered by a single NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano module.
- **VoltEdge Pro** — the mid-range workhorse, built around dual Jetson Orin NX
  modules with hardware video decode for up to 16 concurrent camera streams.
- **VoltEdge Max** — rack-mounted unit with a single NVIDIA L4 GPU for
  high-density multi-tenant inference.

All variants ship with TensorRT-optimized model runtimes and serve models
through NVIDIA Triton Inference Server. Supported model formats are ONNX,
TensorRT engines, and TorchScript. Customers manage fleets through the
Kestrel Cloud console.
